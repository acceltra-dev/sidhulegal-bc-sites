Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Sidhu Legal ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a contact or case review form on our website
  • Call or email our office
  • Book a consultation

This may include your name, phone number, email address, and a description of your legal matter.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and assess your legal matter
  • Schedule and conduct consultations
  • Communicate with you about your case if you become a client
  • Comply with our legal and professional obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Solicitor-Client Privilege

Information you share with us in the context of seeking legal advice is protected by solicitor-client privilege. This is one of the strongest privacy protections available under Canadian law.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This data is aggregated and anonymous — it does not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law and our professional obligations as lawyers.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information held by us. To make a request, contact us at info@ngsidhu.com.
